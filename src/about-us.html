Vision

To empower our clients with the most comprehensive and accurate financial data & analytics, helping them make informed decisions and achieve their goals.

Mission

WVB specialises in the worldwide collection of timely, comprehensive, and transparent company financial information providing our customers with the essential data to make sound financial decisions. As the last remaining independent, privately owned, company providing global coverage in this field, our aim is to provide an even and stable playing field to ALL market participants in need of global financial data.

Economic Vision

To operate on a sustainable financial basis of profitable growth, to increase value for our stakeholders and business partners, and to expand opportunities for development and career growth for our employees in a respectful and tolerant atmosphere.

Social Mission

To operate in a way that actively recognizes the business's central role in society by initiating innovative ways to improve the quality of life locally, nationally & internationally.
