Why choose WVB?

Product and services of Highest QUALITY

Client First

Clients are always at the center of any business. As a leading financial data provider, our vision extends beyond our immediate customers. We always start with your requirements and think from the perspective of you as end customers, the areas of pain, and we design a solution that solves important challenges to aid your business.

Data coverage

Data that is easily accessible allows you to move quickly and create a data-informed culture in which data leads to better decisions and actions. We have a global coverage of financial data, with over 54,000 active publicly traded companies collected from 160 countries to fulfil your requirements.

Customization

If the underlying data were made easily accessible, data users might choose the weights they deem appropriate for their objectives by moduler, tailoring the design solution to your company requirements.

Global Data Consistency

WVB started collecting data initially from hard-to-get countries and companies and later moved to G24 countries. This results in a unique product that allows you to easily do your peer group analysis including small countries industries i.e. Bhutan with US industries. The depth and breadth of data are the same as you would expect for a G24 country.

Timely Delivery
WVB has used a "fast track method" as well as a pro-active collection strategy since 1999. Data from the RNS is entered the same day it is published.
Generally, press release information is recorded within 24 hours of publication. Once received, annual reports are ranked and updated within 48 hours during off-peak times and one week during high times.
WVB classifies enterprises based on market capitalization once a year (at the conclusion of the fiscal year). The firms are then divided into three groups: priority one, priority two, priority three.
Quality Control

We keep the source (PDF) on hand because it is crucial for a through quality check of the data; for example, if a client comes back to WVB having doubts about certain data items, the source report serves us as prove and quality check

Result Focused

We develop adaptable products, not just functionality. In the long term, our priority is making your company succeed with well informed financial data.

World' Vest Base (WVB) is a primary source for global financial, statistical and market information.
The database universe spans over 54,000 active, publicly-listed companies collected from over 160 countries.
